About the App

「我也會讀題目」is a fun, interactive Chinese vocabulary learning app for children ages 3–12. Kids learn essential Math and Reading Comprehension words through audio-based quizzes and illustrated flashcards, covering Preschool through Primary 6.

Features

  • 60+ vocabulary words across 7 grade levels
  • Learn mode with pinyin, audio, and example sentences
  • Quiz mode — listen and match the correct character
  • Supports Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, and English
  • Child-friendly design with animations and confetti rewards

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is the app free?
A: Yes! The app is free to download and use. It is supported by ads.

Q: What ages is it for?
A: The app is designed for children ages 3–12, from Preschool to Primary 6.

Q: Does it work offline?
A: The core learning and quiz features work offline. Text-to-speech requires an internet connection on some devices.

Q: Which devices are supported?
A: The app is available on iPhone and iPad running iOS 16 or later.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how "I Can Read Questions" (我也會讀題目) (the "App"), developed by KSN, handles information when you use the App. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children.

Effective Date: April 1, 2026

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. The App does not require an account, login, or registration of any kind. No names, email addresses, photos, or location data are ever requested or gathered.

Children's Privacy

The App is designed for children ages 3–12. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

Third-Party Services — Advertising

The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect and use certain data (such as device identifiers, IP address, and general usage data) to serve ads. For users in the EEA/UK, consent is requested before personalised ads are shown.

For children or users who have not consented to personalised advertising, ads are served in a limited, non-personalised manner in compliance with Google's policies.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from apps.

Text-to-Speech

The App uses on-device text-to-speech to play audio pronunciations. On some devices this feature may require an internet connection. No voice data is recorded or transmitted by the App.

Data Storage

All learning data (such as quiz progress) is stored locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers by the App itself.

Your Choices

You can manage your ad personalisation preferences within the App or through your device settings. See the User Privacy Choices tab for details.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Overview

"I Can Read Questions" respects your privacy. This page explains the choices you have regarding data collection and advertising within the App.

Ad Personalisation

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. You have the following options regarding ad personalisation:

Opt Out of Personalised Ads (iOS)

  1. Open your device Settings
  2. Go to Privacy & Security → Tracking
  3. Disable Allow Apps to Request to Track, or turn off tracking for this App specifically

You can also limit ad tracking under Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → turn off Personalised Ads.

Google Ad Settings

You can manage Google's ad personalisation settings at:
https://adssettings.google.com

App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS 14.5 and later, the App will request your permission before tracking your activity across other companies' apps and websites. You can change this at any time in your device settings under Privacy & Security → Tracking.

If you deny tracking, ads will still be displayed but they will be non-personalised.

Data Collected by Third Parties

The App itself does not collect personal data. However, Google AdMob, as a third-party advertising service, may collect:

  • Device identifiers (e.g. IDFA on iOS)
  • IP address
  • General usage and interaction data
  • Crash and diagnostic data

This data is handled according to Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Deletion

Since the App does not collect or store personal data on any server, there is no account data to delete. All learning progress is stored locally on your device and can be removed by uninstalling the App.

https://adssettings.google.com
